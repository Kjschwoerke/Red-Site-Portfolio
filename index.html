So...Who exactly am I?

I am an artist and former counselor turned web developer with a passion for the user experience. I spend a lot of my time researching current UI and UX design trends, and enjoy finding creative ways to apply these trends as well as classic design in my projects.

While I am most passionate about the front end of web development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React. I am also familiar with working on the back end of projects specifically with Node.js and Express.js.

When I am not coding or doing code related activities. I greatly enjoy spending time outside hiking or biking and inside working on my latest art project, Warhammer diorama, reading, or playing video games.
